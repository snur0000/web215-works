Mission

Sabby's Skate Shop is a locally owned skate shop company based in Charlotte, North Carolina that has all your skateboarding needs!! We have decks in all sizes and shapes ranging from 7.5 - 8.9, trucks, bearings, wheels, etc, and a wide range of skate apparal and your favorite skate shoe brands from vans to nike sb.

We carry an amazing selection of decks from pro models from your favorite brands to locally designed and produced boards. We believe skateboarding is for EVERYBODY and strive to be inclusive by offering a wide selection for our different customer's. Come by and visit us and we will get you right :), We are open Monday - Friday from 10am - 5pm. Hope to see you soon!
